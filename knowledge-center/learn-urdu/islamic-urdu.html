Islamic Vocabulary

Islamic Terms in Urdu

Essential words you will encounter frequently in Urdu Islamic lectures, books, and articles.

نماز
Namaz
Prayer (Salah)
The five daily prayers — called Salah in Arabic, Namaz in Urdu/Persian
روزہ
Roza
Fasting (Sawm)
Fasting in Ramadan — Urdu word for Sawm. Roza rakhna = to keep a fast
زکات
Zakat
Obligatory Charity
The third pillar of Islam — annual charity on wealth
حج
Hajj
Pilgrimage
The pilgrimage to Makkah — fifth pillar of Islam
تفسیر
Tafseer
Quranic Exegesis
Commentary and explanation of the Quran — vast tradition in Urdu
حدیث
Hadith
Prophetic Traditions
Sayings and actions of the Prophet ﷺ — extensively documented in Urdu
فقہ
Fiqh
Islamic Jurisprudence
Islamic law and rulings — major Urdu books exist in all four madhabs
اجتہاد
Ijtihad
Independent Legal Reasoning
Scholarly effort to derive rulings from Quran and Sunnah
شریعت
Shariah
Islamic Law/Path
The complete way of life ordained by Allah for Muslims
سنت
Sunnah
Prophetic Practice
The way of the Prophet ﷺ — his practices that Muslims follow
دار العلوم
Darul Uloom
House of Knowledge
Islamic seminary — famously Darul Uloom Deoband in India
علماء
Ulama
Islamic Scholars
Plural of Alim — learned Islamic scholars
مفتی
Mufti
Islamic Jurist
Scholar qualified to issue Islamic legal opinions (fatawa)
آخرت
Aakhirat
The Hereafter
Life after death — a central concern in Islamic teaching
دعوت
Dawat
Invitation to Islam
Calling people to Islam — Dawat-e-Tabligh is a major Urdu movement
تبلیغ
Tabligh
Propagation of Islam
Spreading the message of Islam — Tabligh Jamaat operates in Urdu
رمضان
Ramadan
Month of Fasting
The blessed ninth month of the Islamic calendar
صدقہ
Sadaqah
Voluntary Charity
Voluntary giving for the sake of Allah — sadaqah jariyah = ongoing charity
توحید
Tawheed
Oneness of Allah
The fundamental Islamic belief in the absolute oneness of Allah
مسجد
Masjid
Mosque
Place of prostration — the center of Muslim community life
Dars and Bayan Phrases

Phrases from Urdu Islamic Lectures

Key phrases you will hear in every Urdu dars and bayan — begin to understand the language of Islamic lectures.

اللہ تعالیٰ فرماتے ہیں
Allah Ta'ala farmatay hain
Allah says... (used before citing the Quran)
Introduces a Quranic verse
نبی کریم ﷺ نے ارشاد فرمایا
Nabi Kareem ﷺ ne irshad farmaya
The noble Prophet ﷺ said... (before citing a hadith)
Introduces a hadith narration
بھائیو اور بہنو!
Bhaiyo aur behnon!
Brothers and sisters! (Common address in Urdu lectures)
Heard in virtually every Urdu lecture
اللہ ہمیں توفیق دے
Allah hamein taufeeq dey
May Allah give us the ability to act on this
A frequent dua in Urdu lectures
اللہ تعالیٰ ہم سب کی مغفرت فرمائے
Allah Ta'ala hum sab ki maghfirat farmaey
May Allah forgive all of us
Often heard at the close of a lecture
صلوٰۃ و سلام اس نبی ﷺ پر
Salawaat wa salaam us nabi ﷺ par
Blessings and peace upon that Prophet ﷺ
Said when the Prophet's name is mentioned
Urdu Islamic Literature

Key Urdu Islamic Books

Urdu holds a unique place in Islamic literature. These books are milestones to work toward as your reading improves.

ف

Fazail-e-Amal

Maulana Zakariyya Kandhlawi

A widely read Urdu book on the virtues and importance of Islamic practices.

Devotional
ت

Tafheem ul Quran

Syed Abul Ala Maududi

A comprehensive Urdu tafseer of the Quran — one of the most widely read Urdu Islamic works.

Tafseer
ب

Bahishti Zewar

Maulana Ashraf Ali Thanvi

A comprehensive Urdu guide on Islamic practices, fiqh, and ethics.

Fiqh
س

Seerat un-Nabi ﷺ

Allama Shibli Nomani

A monumental Urdu work on the life of the Prophet ﷺ.

Seerah
ر

Riyad us-Saliheen (Urdu)

Imam Nawawi (Urdu Translation)

Imam Nawawi's classic hadith collection — widely read in Urdu translation.

Hadith
ن

Nafahat ul Quran

Maulana Tariq Jameel

A modern Urdu work by one of the most widely listened Urdu Islamic scholars.

Contemporary
📖

First Urdu book recommendation: Fazail-e-Amal or Bahishti Zewar — both use accessible Urdu. Read one page per day to build fluency steadily.
