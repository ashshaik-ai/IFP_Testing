# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Telugu-speaking Muslim community and the wider Mangalagiri electorate in Andhra Pradesh. They meet this brand on mobile: WhatsApp forwards and statuses, Facebook and Instagram posts, and the static website (default Telugu, English toggle). Older readers and print (flex banners) matter — text must stay large and unambiguous.

## Product Purpose

Communications for Islamic Front, Mangalagiri — a community-welfare and political organization led by Shaik Akram (AP State Waqf Board Director, Islamic Front Convener, MHPS State Secretary). Surfaces: the multilingual static site, Telugu poster/banner output (posters/), and Telugu PDF brochures. Success = material that community members forward proudly and that reads as professionally designed by a human.

## Brand Personality

Dignified, trustworthy, rooted. Premium political/social-organization voice — never flashy, never cluttered. Deep green identity with restrained gold; the founder's face and the party emblem are the recurring anchors.

## Anti-references

- AI-looking layouts: random decorative shapes, glow effects, neon colors, heavy gradients, generic poster clutter (banned in Telugu_Poster_Framework.md).
- Cheap template posters from poster-maker apps: identical layouts with swapped photos.
- Anything that misrenders Telugu: text must be real shaped text, never AI-painted glyphs.

## Design Principles

1. Zones before styling — photo, text, footer, decoration each get hard reserved space; overlap is never resolved with z-index.
2. Text is sacred — exact name/designation blocks, verified Telugu spelling and grammar, no letterspacing on Telugu.
3. One family, committed contrast — Noto Sans Telugu across weights 400–900 carries the hierarchy; a second family only for Latin abbreviations.
4. Restrained gold on deep green — gold is an accent for the headline and identity moments, not a wash.
5. Human QA gate — every deliverable passes the framework checklist at full size before it ships.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Site: WCAG 2.2 AA (contrast 4.5:1, focus visible, semantic HTML). Posters: mobile-first readability (legible at WhatsApp thumbnail size), high contrast for older readers, print-safe at flex-banner scale.
